ABOUT THE ASEAN INVESTMENT CHALLENGE

The ASEAN Investment Challenge (AIC) is CGS International’s flagship youth programme to empower students with knowledge in sustainable investing and contribute to the building of strong capital markets in the future. The regional challenge equips tertiary students with lifelong skills in personal finance management and knowledge in sustainable investing. In short, think of it as a platform to propel yourself towards financial independence!

Here's what participants in AIC 2025 can look forward to:

  • Deep insights in capital markets investing from industry experts from CGS International and local stock exchanges
  • Access to CGS Academy - an e-learning platform dedicated to providing a holistic learning experience on topics such as Basic Financial Literacy, Technical Analysis, and Sustainable Finance
  • Hands-on practice with a demo trading account to hone investment skills, and compete for attractive cash prizes totalling more than $36,000

The ASEAN Investment Challenge Winner

AIC is designed to recognise the best and bravest in sustainable investing: Build your knowledge and hone your investment skills across a wide range of topics, and put them to practice through the demo trading account. [The ones who achieve the highest returns win!]If you have what it takes to come up tops in both the Country and Regional Challenges, you will bring home over $36,000++ in cash to kickstart your financial journey.

The Path To Victory

AIC participants will put their trading skills to the test at the Country Level, where they will compete against other participants in the same country. Participants must trade a minimum of three unique local stocks available across the selected markets. The top 3 participants [with the highest returns] from each country will then advance to the Regional Challenge for an opportunity to be crowned the overall winner.


Oh, and did we mention the total of S$36,000++ in cash that our winners stand to bring home? What are you waiting for? JOIN NOW!
